Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service. This includes event codes entered by guests, and basic usage data such as page visits. We do not require account registration or collect personal details from guests.

2. Photos & Content

Photos are uploaded by photographers and are accessible only to guests with the correct event code. We do not share, sell, or distribute event photos to third parties. Photos are stored securely and deleted after the event expiry period.

3. Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage to remember your last accessed event code, dark mode preference, and favourited photos. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Bootstrap and Bootstrap Icons via CDN (jsDelivr). When you use social share buttons (WhatsApp, Facebook), you are redirected to those platforms and their privacy policies apply.

5. Data Retention

Event galleries are available for 90 days after the event by default. After expiry, all associated photos are permanently deleted. Photographers may request extended retention periods.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@eventgallery.com.
