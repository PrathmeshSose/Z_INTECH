Who We Are

Our Mission

Event Gallery was built to solve a simple problem — sharing event photos privately and easily. Whether it's a wedding, birthday, corporate event, graduation, or concert, every guest deserves quick and easy access to their memories.

Photographers upload photos through our admin panel, and guests access them instantly using a unique event code — no account required.

Our Values

Guest Experience

Simple Process

How It Works

1. Photographer Uploads

The photographer uploads photos via the admin panel after the event.

2. Guest Gets a Code

Each event has a unique code shared with guests by the organizer.

3. View & Download

Guests enter the code, browse the gallery, and download their photos.
